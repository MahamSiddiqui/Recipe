Potato Puff

Recipe by Chef Noshi

Ingredients

  • PotatoÂ½ kg
  • Eggs 2
  • All purpose flour 2 tbsp
  • Bread crumbs 6 tsp
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Oil for deep fry

Cooking Instruction

  1. Peel and grate the potato.
  2. Mix in eggs, all purpose flour, bread crumbs, salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a wok and deep fry like fritters until golden.
  4. Serve with ketchup.
